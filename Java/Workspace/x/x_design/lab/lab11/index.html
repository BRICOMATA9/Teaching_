Rooms may be snug, particularly Meldrum 110. If things are just too tight, we will come up with another plan.

Details regarding the presentations will be covered in class, but roughly, please plan on using a whiteboard to summarize your pattern, in particular:

1) The name of the pattern and an overview what it does.

2) A UML overview of the pattern

3) Where it may be used (use a situation different than what we covered in lab.)

4) How it may be related to other patterns ... i.e. "our abc pattern works well with the xyz pattern because"

You may use code snippets to further illustrate your pattern.

Plan on a 5 minute presentation of your pattern.
